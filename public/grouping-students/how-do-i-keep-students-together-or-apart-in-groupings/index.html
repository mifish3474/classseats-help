How do I keep students together or apart in groupings?

Use this when you need to force two students to be placed together (or kept apart) the next time you generate groups.

Add a constraint

  1. Click the Students tab.
  2. Select the class period you want to work with.
  3. In the Constraints section, choose Student A and Student B.
  4. Choose one:
    • Add Together to keep the two students together
    • Add Apart to keep the two students separated
  5. Confirm the pair appears in the list below (Must work together or Must stay apart).

Remove a constraint

  1. Find the pair you want to remove in the list.
  2. Click the X next to the constraint.
Tip: Constraints affect group generation. If your groups are already created, regenerate groups to apply the change.
