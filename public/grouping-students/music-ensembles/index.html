How do I set up music ensembles?

Use tags and grouping options to quickly build music ensembles such as sectionals, quartets, or mixed-instrument groups.

Before you start

Decide whether you want to group students by instrument section, by skill level, or by a mix of both.

If needed, update student names first so your roster is accurate before generating ensembles.

Step 1: Add music tags to students

  1. Open the Students tab for the correct class period.
  2. Add tags for each student (for example: Soprano, Alto, Tenor, Bass, or instrument names).
  3. Save your changes after tagging.

Step 2: Configure grouping options

  1. Go to the Groups tab.
  2. Choose the group size and any balancing preferences you want to enforce.
  3. Select criteria that use your student tags when building groups.

Step 3: Generate and review ensembles

  1. Click Preview to review a proposed grouping.
  2. Adjust options if the ensemble mix is not what you want.
  3. Click Apply Picks when the grouping looks right.
Tip: If you want a fresh mix for a rehearsal day, use randomize options for one run, then switch back to tag-based rules for your next formal ensemble setup.
