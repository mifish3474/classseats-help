How do I add student photos?

Use this when you want to take and save student photos using ClassSeats Mobile.

Open the correct class

  1. Open ClassSeats Mobile on your phone or tablet.
  2. Make sure you are connected to Google Drive.
  3. Load the file and class you want to add photos to.

Take a student photo

  1. Find the student you want to photograph.
  2. Tap the camera icon next to the student’s name.
  3. Frame the shot (a shoulders-up headshot works best).
  4. Tap Take photo.
  5. Choose Retake to try again, or Accept to save the photo.

Where the photo is used and stored

  • The photo becomes available everywhere photos are used in ClassSeats (desktop and mobile).
  • Photos are saved to your Google Drive in the ClassSeats/studentPhotos folder, using the student’s name.
Tip: Use good lighting and a plain background for clearer, more consistent student photos.
