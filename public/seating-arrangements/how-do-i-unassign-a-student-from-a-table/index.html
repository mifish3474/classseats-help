How do I unassign a student from a table?

Use this when you want to remove a student from a table so they appear in the Unassigned area.

Unassign a student

  1. Open your seating chart (from Groups → View Seating Chart), or view the seating chart while working in Groups.
  2. Hover your mouse over the student you want to unassign.
  3. Click the Unassign button that appears on the student’s card.
  4. Confirm the student appears in the Unassigned area.
