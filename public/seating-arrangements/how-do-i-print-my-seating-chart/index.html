How do I print my seating chart?

Use this when you want a paper copy (or PDF) of your current seating chart for a class period.

Open the Print Layout Editor

  1. Open your seating chart (from Groups → View Seating Chart).
  2. Click Print.
  3. The Print Layout Editor will open with a live preview of your seating chart.

Choose your print settings

  1. Under Page, choose your Paper (for example, Letter).
  2. Choose an Orientation (Portrait or Landscape).
  3. Under View, adjust zoom using - and +.
  4. Click Center to center the layout, or Fit to page to scale it to the page.
  5. Under Content, choose what to include:
    • Show photos
    • Show tags
    • Seat numbers
    • Choose tags… (to print only selected tags)
    • Text size and Photo size

Print

  1. (Optional) Click Save Settings to save your print settings for next time.
  2. Click Save Settings & Print to open your browser’s print dialog.
  3. In the print dialog, complete printing (or save as PDF).
Tip: For the most WYSIWYG results, use Default margins in the print dialog.
