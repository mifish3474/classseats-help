How do I view my seating chart?

Use this when you want to see your students placed into seats based on your current groups and seating arrangement.

View a seating chart

  1. Click the Groups tab in the top navigation.
  2. Select your class period.
  3. If groups have not been created yet, generate groups using Seating Arrangement mode.
  4. At the top of the grouping results, click View Seating Chart.
  5. The seating chart will open using your current arrangement and group assignments.

Adjust what you see

  1. Use the Unassigned Students panel on the left:
    • Leave it open to see students without seats
    • Hide it to see more of the seating chart
  2. Use the zoom controls at the top of the screen to zoom in or out.
  3. Use the toggles at the top to show or hide:
    • Seat numbers
    • Preferred seats
  4. If students have tags, select specific tags to highlight where those students are seated.

Close the seating chart

  1. Click Save & Close to save any changes you’ve made to the seating chart.
  2. Click Close to exit without saving changes.
Tip: The seating chart always reflects your current groups and seating arrangement. If something looks off, double-check that you’re viewing the correct class period and grouping.
